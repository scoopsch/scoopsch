Places that I like

Baguio

Baguio, also known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," is a beautiful city nestled in the mountains of Benguet. It is known for its cool climate, making it a perfect escape from the tropical heat of the lowlands. The city is famous for its stunning parks, such as Burnham Park and Wright Park, where visitors can enjoy boat rides and horseback riding. Baguio is also home to the vibrant Session Road, filled with cafes, restaurants, and local shops. The fresh air and scenic views make every visit a refreshing experience.

Aside from its natural beauty, Baguio is rich in culture and history. The Baguio Cathedral stands as a historical landmark, offering a peaceful place for reflection. Tourists can also explore the BenCab Museum, which showcases Philippine contemporary art and indigenous culture. The city is also known for its unique traditions, such as the Panagbenga Festival, which celebrates the beauty of flowers through grand parades and street dances. These cultural experiences make Baguio more than just a tourist spot—it’s a place with deep-rooted heritage.

Baguio is also a food lover’s paradise. Visitors can indulge in the famous strawberry taho, ube jam from Good Shepherd, and the hearty flavors of Baguio’s local cuisine. The city’s night market along Harrison Road offers affordable street food and thrift shopping, attracting both locals and tourists. With a cozy atmosphere, friendly locals, and breathtaking sceneries, Baguio remains a favorite destination. Whether for relaxation or adventure, this city offers something special for everyone.

Atok

Atok, a hidden gem in Benguet, is a breathtaking destination known for its high-altitude landscapes. Located over 2,000 meters above sea level, it offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. One of its most famous attractions is the Northern Blossom Flower Farm, where visitors can witness vast fields of vibrant flowers, including rare species. The cool weather and misty atmosphere make it a perfect place for nature lovers seeking peace and tranquility. With its scenic beauty and fresh mountain air, Atok provides a unique experience away from the crowded tourist spots.

Atok is also home to the Highest Point of the Philippine Highway System, offering an incredible panoramic view of the Cordillera mountains. The place is ideal for sunrise watching, as the clouds form a sea of mist over the hills. Many travelers visit Atok for its refreshing climate, which is often colder than Baguio. The town also has terraced vegetable farms, supplying fresh produce to different parts of the country. Its natural beauty and serene environment make it a must-visit destination for those who love outdoor adventures.

Despite its rural setting, Atok has a rich history and culture. The indigenous people of the Cordillera region, particularly the Ibaloi, have preserved their traditions and way of life. Visitors can learn about their customs, beliefs, and traditional farming methods. The simplicity and warmth of the locals make Atok a welcoming place for travelers. This hidden paradise proves that some of the most beautiful destinations are those that remain untouched by urbanization.

South Korea

South Korea is a country known for its rich culture, modern cities, and breathtaking landscapes. From the bustling streets of Seoul to the peaceful countryside of Jeju Island, South Korea offers a diverse range of experiences. The country is famous for its historical palaces, such as Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung, which showcase the elegance of traditional Korean architecture. Tourists can also explore the vibrant neighborhoods of Hongdae and Myeongdong, known for their shopping, street food, and entertainment. With its mix of history and modernity, South Korea has something for every traveler.

Korean culture has gained global recognition through K-pop, K-dramas, and Korean cuisine. Fans from all over the world visit South Korea to experience the Hallyu wave, visiting entertainment companies, themed cafés, and filming locations. Food lovers can enjoy authentic Korean dishes like kimchi, samgyeopsal, and tteokbokki at local markets and restaurants. The country also has unique cultural experiences, such as wearing a hanbok and participating in traditional tea ceremonies. These aspects make South Korea a fascinating destination filled with tradition and innovation.

Aside from its urban attractions, South Korea is home to stunning natural wonders. Nami Island, with its picturesque tree-lined paths, is a romantic getaway for visitors. Jeju Island offers breathtaking beaches, volcanic landscapes, and scenic waterfalls, perfect for adventure seekers. During spring, the country is adorned with cherry blossoms, creating a magical atmosphere. Whether exploring ancient temples or high-tech cities, South Korea provides a unique and unforgettable travel experience.

Intramuros

Intramuros, the historic walled city of Manila, is a must-visit for those who want to explore the Philippines' colonial past. Built during the Spanish era, it served as the center of administration, education, and religion. The massive stone walls and cobblestone streets take visitors back in time, giving them a glimpse of the country's history. One of its main attractions is Fort Santiago, a fortress that once served as a prison for national hero José Rizal. Walking through Intramuros feels like stepping into a different era, where every corner has a story to tell.

The city is also home to several historical landmarks, including San Agustin Church, the oldest stone church in the Philippines. Visitors can explore Casa Manila, a museum that showcases Spanish colonial-era lifestyle and architecture. The Manila Cathedral stands as a symbol of faith and resilience, having been rebuilt multiple times due to wars and natural disasters. Kalesa (horse-drawn carriage) rides offer a unique way to tour around the city and learn more about its history. These well-preserved landmarks make Intramuros a living museum of the Philippines' past.

Beyond its historical sites, Intramuros has a lively cultural scene. Local cafes and restaurants offer traditional Filipino cuisine, while art galleries and street performances showcase Filipino talent. At night, the city lights up, giving it a charming ambiance perfect for evening walks. Intramuros is also known for educational tours, making it a favorite destination for students and history enthusiasts. With its timeless beauty and historical significance, it remains one of the most iconic places in the Philippines.

Switzerland

Switzerland is a dream destination known for its breathtaking landscapes and picturesque towns. It is home to the stunning Swiss Alps, offering world-class skiing, hiking, and scenic train rides. The country is famous for its pristine lakes, such as Lake Geneva and Lake Lucerne, where visitors can enjoy boat rides and lakeside views. Switzerland’s charming cities, including Zurich, Bern, and Geneva, offer a mix of modern attractions and medieval architecture. With its beautiful scenery and peaceful atmosphere, Switzerland is truly a paradise for nature lovers.

Aside from its natural beauty, Switzerland is known for its rich culture and traditions. The country is famous for its delicious chocolates and cheeses, which can be found in markets and specialty shops. Swiss watches are also world-renowned, symbolizing precision and craftsmanship. Festivals like the Montreux Jazz Festival and Fasnacht in Basel bring people together to celebrate music, arts, and heritage. These cultural elements make Switzerland more than just a scenic destination—it’s also a place of artistic and culinary excellence.

Switzerland is also one of the most peaceful and safest countries in the world. It is known for its efficient public transportation system, making it easy for travelers to explore different regions. The country’s neutrality and high quality of life attract visitors from all over the world. Whether enjoying a cup of hot chocolate in the Alps or strolling through historic streets, every moment in Switzerland feels magical. With its stunning landscapes and welcoming atmosphere, Switzerland remains one of the most sought-after travel destinations.
